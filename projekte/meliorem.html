I was given the task of programming a website with a log-in button, this was something I had never done before. That is why I researched online how a log-in button works. Then I thought about the concept of my website. I thought I had a good idea, a website where you could create a to-do-list and design it the way you like best. It would also be able to remind you what you still have to do, or if you set a date you can see your tasks in a calendar layout which would help you stay up to date.
